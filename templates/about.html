INTRODUCTION

Gong Cha was officially put into operation in Vietnam market by Golden Trust Co., Ltd., in October 11st, 2014. Over five years of operation, Golden Trust Co., Ltd.- the Master of Franchise of Gong Cha in Vietnam, has made the brand grow expeditiously and become one of the most favorite F&B brands for young generation who loves milk tea and has the desire to experience qualified tea products from only selected ingredients.
